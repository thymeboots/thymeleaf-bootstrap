Row

Break a new row

The <tb:row> tag is a container you can put html code in.

Example

1 of 2 2 of 2 1 of 2 2 of 2
Html markup
  <tb:container>
    <tb:row>
      <tb:column>
         1 of 2
      </tb:column>
      <tb:column>
         2 of 2
      </tb:column>
    </tb:row>
    <tb:row>
      <tb:column>
         1 of 2
      </tb:column>
      <tb:column>
         2 of 2
      </tb:column>
    </tb:row>
  </tb:container>
Attribute Default value Description
align align-items: [start,center,end]
justify justify-content: [start,center,end,around,between,evenly]
